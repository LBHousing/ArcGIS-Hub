The funnel · all years

Restoration = the lockout. When the sheriff records a “Restoration,” possession is restored to the landlord and the tenant is displaced — it is the completed eviction, not a tenant win. The gap between notices and lockouts is the households that left, settled, or were pushed out before the sheriff returned.
