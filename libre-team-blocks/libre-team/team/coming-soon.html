The LiBRE + CSULB Collaboration

Since 2024, LiBRE has partnered with California State University, Long Beach to build a first-of-its-kind housing data platform for the city. What began as a collaborative GIS mapping project has grown into a comprehensive public resource — combining Census data, LA County Assessor records, eviction filings, and housing violation data into interactive dashboards and analysis tools.

CSULB students and faculty have contributed research, data analysis, and technical development across multiple academic terms, working alongside LiBRE staff to ensure the platform serves the community members who need it most. This partnership demonstrates how academic institutions and grassroots organizations can collaborate to produce tools that advance housing justice and informed policymaking.
