Housing is a human right.
We have the data to prove

LiBRE uses GIS technology and public data to map the forces displacing Long Beach renters — from corporate landlords and code violations to rent burden and eviction patterns. In a city that's 60% renters, understanding who owns the housing is the first step to protecting the people who live in it.
