Know Your Rights

Of the city's 186,658 dwelling units, tens of thousands of renters are covered by CTPA — but many don't know it. Long Beach also has additional local protections under the Just Cause Termination Ordinance (LBMC §8.99).

If you believe your rights have been violated:

→ Use tenantprotections.org/calculator to check if a rent increase is legal

→ Contact LiBRE for guidance and Know Your Rights workshops

→ Connect with a free attorney through Stay Housed LA

→ Visit LAFLA (Legal Aid Foundation of Los Angeles) for low-income legal help

→ Learn about the Block the Cuts and Build Long Beach Ordinance →
